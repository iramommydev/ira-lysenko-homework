The practice of baby-led weaning allows baby to self-feed finger foods as their first solid food rather than the more traditional rice, oatmeal or fruit and vegetable purees. Baby-led weaning is a method for introducing solid foods that doesn't involve purees or spoon feeding. Instead, your baby feeds herself soft foods that you place in front of her.
